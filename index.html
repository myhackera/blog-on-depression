DEPRESSION
: WORLD LARGEST HEALTH PROBLEM

Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest. Also called major depressive disorder or clinical depression, it affects how you feel, think and behave and can lead to a variety of emotional and physical problems. You may have trouble doing normal day-to-day activities, and sometimes you may feel as if life isn't worth living.

DEPRESSION IN INDIA:

Depression is more common in women than men. Nearly every thirteenth person in India runs at the risk of developing an episode of depression during his lifetime. As per WHO, the average age of depression in India is 31.9 years. During corona phase , due to lockdown there is rapid growth of depression cases in india with a rate of 43% . Losing of jobs and decli ned finance is another reason of deterioration of mental health .Many studies indicates that an increasing number of people acrooss the country are dealing with mental health issues triggered by the spread of coronavirus and the consequent lockdown.

SYMPTOMS:

1. Feeling sad or empty
2. Feeling hopeless or helpless
3. Feeling worthless
4. Feeling excessively guilty
5. No interest or pleasure in activities
6. Anger and irritability
7. Feeling tired and a loss of energy
8. Insomnia or lack of sleep
9. Difficulty concentrating, remembering, and making decisions
10. Lack of appetite
11. Overeating and weight gain
12. Aches, pains, and physical symptoms
13. Thoughts of death or suicide

TREATEMENT:

Some people with depression find that the following measures can help them manage their symptoms:
1.) exercising, which can be as effective as medication in some cases
2.) spending time with supportive friends or loved ones
3.) abstaining from alcohol and illicit drugs
4.) trying stress management techniques, such as yoga or journaling
5.) avoiding taking herbs or supplements without talking with a doctor, as some may interfere with antidepressants
6.) breaking significant tasks down into smaller ones and only doing those that are a priority
